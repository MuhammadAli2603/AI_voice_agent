TechStore Return and Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

General Return Policy:
TechStore accepts returns within 30 days of the original purchase date for most products. Items must be returned in their original condition with all accessories, manuals, and packaging materials. Products showing signs of use, damage, or missing components may not be eligible for full refund and may be subject to a restocking fee.

Initiating a Return:
To start a return, log into your TechStore account and navigate to the Order History section. Select the order containing the item you wish to return and click the "Return Item" button. You will be guided through the return process and can print a prepaid return shipping label. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team at 1-800-TECH-HELP for assistance.

Refund Processing:
Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it within 2-3 business days. If the return is approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been issued. Please note that it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account depending on your financial institution.

Non-Returnable Items:
Certain items are not eligible for return due to hygiene, safety, or licensing reasons. These include:
- Software with broken seals or activated license keys
- Personal care items (electric shavers, earbuds with opened ear tips)
- Digital downloads and subscriptions
- Gift cards
- Items marked as final sale or clearance
- Custom-built or personalized products

Please check the product page before purchasing to verify the return eligibility.

Defective or Damaged Items:
If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will provide a prepaid return label and send a replacement at no additional cost. For defects discovered after the 30-day return window but within the warranty period, please refer to the manufacturer's warranty information included with your product.

Restocking Fees:
A 15% restocking fee may apply to certain returns, including:
- Opened software or gaming products
- Special order items
- Large appliances (over 50 pounds)
- Items returned after 15 days but within the 30-day window
- Items with missing accessories or damaged packaging

Restocking fees will be deducted from your refund amount.

Holiday Returns:
Items purchased between November 1 and December 25 have an extended return period through January 31 of the following year. All other return policy terms and conditions still apply.

TechStore Shipping Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Shipping Methods and Costs:
TechStore offers multiple shipping options to meet your needs:

- Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99
- Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $12.99
- Overnight Shipping (next business day): $24.99
- Store Pickup: Free (ready in 2-4 hours)

Shipping costs are calculated based on order value and destination. International shipping rates vary by location and are calculated at checkout.

Order Processing:
Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days are usually processed the same day. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day. You will receive an email confirmation when your order is placed and another email with tracking information once your order ships.

Shipping Restrictions:
We ship to all 50 US states, APO/FPO addresses, and select international locations. Some products may have shipping restrictions due to size, weight, or battery regulations. Large items such as TVs and appliances may require special delivery arrangements. International orders may be subject to customs fees, duties, and taxes which are the responsibility of the customer.

Delivery Requirements:
A signature may be required for high-value orders (over $500) or certain product categories. If no one is available to sign, the carrier will leave a notice with instructions for redelivery or pickup. We are not responsible for packages left unattended per your delivery instructions.

Lost or Damaged Shipments:
If your tracking information shows the package was delivered but you did not receive it, please check with neighbors and household members first. Contact the shipping carrier using your tracking number to file a claim. If the carrier confirms the package is lost or stolen, contact TechStore within 7 days and we will send a replacement or issue a refund.

For packages that arrive damaged, refuse delivery if possible or note the damage with the delivery driver. Take photos of the damaged package and contact us within 48 hours. We will arrange for a replacement to be sent immediately.

International Shipping:
International orders are shipped via USPS, FedEx, or DHL depending on destination. Delivery times vary from 7-21 business days. All international packages include tracking and insurance. Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees assessed by their country. TechStore is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing.

TechStore Warranty Information

Manufacturer Warranties:
All products sold by TechStore include the manufacturer's standard warranty. Warranty terms vary by manufacturer and product category but typically include:

- Computers and laptops: 1 year
- Smartphones and tablets: 1 year
- TVs and displays: 1-2 years
- Accessories: 90 days to 1 year

Manufacturer warranties cover defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. They do not cover accidental damage, misuse, unauthorized modifications, or normal wear and tear.

To file a manufacturer warranty claim, contact the manufacturer directly using the warranty information included with your product. You will typically need proof of purchase, which can be obtained from your TechStore order history.

Extended Warranty Plans:
TechStore offers optional extended warranty plans that provide coverage beyond the manufacturer's warranty:

Standard Protection Plan:
- Extends coverage for an additional 1-2 years beyond manufacturer warranty
- Covers manufacturer defects and malfunctions
- Free shipping for repairs
- Available for most products under $2000
- Cost: 8-15% of product price depending on product category

Premium Protection Plan:
- All benefits of Standard Protection
- Covers accidental damage including drops, spills, and cracks
- No deductibles
- Replacement guarantee (if we can't fix it, we'll replace it)
- 24/7 customer support
- Available for products $100-$5000
- Cost: 12-20% of product price depending on product category

Extended warranty plans must be purchased within 30 days of product purchase. Plans are non-transferable and non-refundable after 30 days.

TechStore Return Warranty:
In addition to manufacturer warranties, TechStore provides additional protection:

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee: Return any product within 30 days if you're not completely satisfied (see Return Policy for details).

Dead on Arrival (DOA) Protection: If a product doesn't work when you first receive it, contact us within 7 days for a free replacement with expedited shipping.

Price Match Guarantee: If you find a lower price from an authorized retailer within 14 days of purchase, we'll refund the difference.
